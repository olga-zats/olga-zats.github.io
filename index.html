Olga Zatsarynna

I'm a PhD student at the University of Bonn, Germany in the Computer Vision group led by Prof. Dr. Juergen Gall . Previously, I completed my Masters degree at the University of Bonn, Germany under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Juergen Gall and my Bachelors degree in the Kharkiv National Karazin University, Ukraine under the supervision of Dr. Pavlo Barkhayev.

My current research focuses on video understanding. More concretely, I am interested in such such topics as: long-term and short-term action forecasting, temporal action segmentation, unintentional action prediction. Previously, I also worked on the task of semantic segmentation.
